Integrative Week Project

November 5, 2014

Project Details:

Welcome to the integrative week assignment. This week is an opportunity to take all the various skills from your classes and put them together into a cohesive project, seeing how HTML, javascript, design, motion and process interrelate into a finished product. Within a team of three to for (randomly assigned) you select a client to work from the list provided. You will be going through a whole project process from research, ideation, sketching, prototyping and creation.
